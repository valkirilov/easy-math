Let's play Timelimit mode of easy-math

Goal

In Timelimit mode your goal is to correctly compare as much more numbers as you can before the deadline time limit of 30 seconds expires. During this time you have to achieve the highest possible score.

Warning: Every correct answer gives you +10 points and a bonus of +0.5 seconds to your time but the incrrect answers coasts you -1 second from your time and -5 points from your score so be careful and don't make mistakes.


You've got 30 secs to show the best of you. Good luck!
