Let's play Classic mode of easy-math

Goal

In Classic mode your goal is to correctly compare as much more numbers as you can to achieve the highest possible score. You have 1.30 minutes to do this so you have to be fast.

Warning: Every correct answer gives you +10 points but the incrrect answers coasts you -1 second from your time and -5 points from your score so be careful and don't make mistakes.
